Vera's peakbagging shenanegans

Hey friends! My name is Veronica Ashcroft (she/her), though I'm also known by my nickname, Vera! I'm a transgender hiker and aspiring mountaineer who does a lot of peakbagging. My signature project is my efforts to take a transgender pride flag up as many mountains as I can! Last year, I hiked my flag up 115 separate mountains in the Northeastern USA 4000fters list, and this spring I worked my way through the New Southeast 202 list! Currently, I'm working through a list of some of the most prominent mountains in the Northern Appalachians, the New Northeast 131!!! If you wanna keep up with me as I go through this project, feel free to follow me on either my tumblr, reddit or insta (verana115, u/verana115 and @sapphaverana respectively). This website is my attempt to kind-of blog my way though the hikes that I do for these projects, along with other trips that I find myself on that I wanna share. In its current state, the website is pretty basic, but that's okay, because learning takes time! As I spend more time on this website, it will get bigger and better, but for now feel free to enjoy what trip reports I have up! And if you want to reach out to me, feel free to message me on either tumblr, reddit or insta!
